
# Kannada Compound Verbs: A Guide to Koḷḷu, Koḍu, and Biḍu

## 1. Overview

In Kannada, just like in Hindi (*Lenā/Denā/Jānā*), auxiliary verbs are attached to the **Past Participle** of a main verb to add nuance to the action. These are often called "Vector Verbs."

**The Golden Formula:**
`Main Verb (Past Participle)` + `Auxiliary Verb (Conjugated)`

---

## 2. The Big Three: Self, Others, and Completion

### A. The Reflexive/Self-Benefactive: ಕೊಳ್ಳು (Koḷḷu)
* **Hindi Equivalent:** लेना (*Lenā*) - To take
* **Function:** Indicates the action is done **for oneself** or directed inwards.

| Root Verb | Past Participle | + Koḷḷu (Written) | + Koḷḷu (Spoken) | Meaning | Hindi Eq. |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Māḍu** (To do) | **Māḍi** | **Māḍikoḷḷu** | **Māḍkoḷḷu** | Do it (yourself) | *Kar lenā* |
| **Bare** (To write) | **Baredu** | **Baredukoḷḷu** | **Barkoḷḷu** | Write down (note it) | *Likh lenā* |
| **Tiḷi** (To know) | **Tiḷidu** | **Tiḷidukoḷḷu** | **Tiḷkoḷḷu** | Understand/Grasp | *Samajh lenā* |
| **Tege** (To take) | **Tegedu** | **Tegedukoḷḷu** | **Tagoḷḷu** | To buy/take | *Le lenā* |

### B. The Benefactive: ಕೊಡು (Koḍu)
* **Hindi Equivalent:** देना (*Denā*) - To give
* **Function:** Indicates the action is done **for someone else** or directed outwards.

| Root Verb | Past Participle | + Koḍu (Written) | + Koḍu (Spoken) | Meaning | Hindi Eq. |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Māḍu** (To do) | **Māḍi** | **Māḍikoḍu** | **Māḍkoḍu** | Do it (for someone) | *Kar denā* |
| **Kaḷuhisu** (To send) | **Kaḷuhisi** | **Kaḷuhisikoḍu** | **Kalskoḍu** | Send it (out) | *Bhej denā* |
| **Hēḷu** (To say) | **Hēḷi** | **Hēḷikoḍu** | **Hēḷkoḍu** | Teach (Say to someone) | *Bata denā* |

### C. The Completive: ಬಿಡು (Biḍu)
* **Hindi Equivalent:** देना (*Denā*) / डालना (*Ḍālnā*) / चुकना (*Chuknā*)
* **Function:** Indicates finality, relief, or getting something over with.

| Root Verb | Past Participle | + Biḍu (Written) | + Biḍu (Spoken) | Meaning | Hindi Eq. |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Svacchamāḍu** | **Svacchamāḍi** | **Svacchamāḍibiḍu** | **Svacchamāḍbiḍu** | Clean it up (finish it) | *Sāf kar do* |
| **Bare** (To write) | **Baredu** | **Baredubiḍu** | **Bardbiḍu** | Write it off/finish writing | *Likh ḍālo* |

## 3. The Koṇḍu Rule: Stative Verbs (Body Posture)

Unlike Hindi, where you use the Past Participle + To Be (*Baitha hoon*), Kannada requires the reflexive **Koṇḍu** (having taken) + **Iru** (to be) for body positions.

**Logic:** "I have taken the seated position and I am existing."

* **Formula:** `Root (Past)` + `ಕೊಂಡು (Koṇḍu)` + `ಇದ್ದೇನೆ (Iddēne)`

| Verb | Written Form | Spoken Form (Bengaluru) | Meaning | Hindi |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **To Sit** | **Kuḷitukoṇḍiddēne** | **Kūtkondiddīni** | I am sitting | *Baiṭha hūn* |
| **To Stand** | **Nintukoṇḍiddēne** | **Nintkondiddīni** | I am standing | *Khaḍā hūn* |
| **To Sleep** | **Malagikoṇḍiddēne** | **Malkondiddīni** | I am sleeping | *Soyā hūn* |

## 4. Nuances & False Friends

### Kēḷu (To Ask/Hear) vs. Kēḷikoḷḷu
* **Kēḷu:** Simple act of asking or hearing.
* **Kēḷikoḷḷu:** To Request / To Beg / To Pray (Asking for oneself).
* **Kēḷisikoḷḷu:** To Listen (Causative reflexive - "To make sound heard by oneself").

### Emotions
Emotions are often treated as nouns that you "do onto yourself."
* **To get angry:** ಕೋಪ ಮಾಡಿಕೊಳ್ಳು (**Kōpa māḍikoḷḷu**)
    * *Imperative:* **Kōpa māḍkōbēḍa** (Don't get angry).
* **To hurry:** ಬೇಗ ಮಾಡಿಕೊಳ್ಳು (**Bēga māḍikoḷḷu**).

---

## 5. Spoken Kannada Morphology (The "Squeeze")

In conversational Bengaluru Kannada, the vowel between the Root and the Auxiliary usually disappears (Syncope).

1.  **Kuḷitu** becomes **Kūt-**
2.  **Malagi** becomes **Mal-**
3.  **Nintu** becomes **Nint-**
4.  **Māḍi** becomes **Māḍ-**
5.  **Tegedu** becomes **Togo-** (Irregular shift)

---

## 6. Conjugation Reference: ಕೊಳ್ಳು (Koḷḷu)

**Root used for example:** ಮಾಡು (*Māḍu* - to do) $\rightarrow$ *Māḍikoḷḷu*

| Tense/Mood | Suffix | Written Example | Spoken Example | Meaning |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Past (I)** | **-koṇḍe** | ಮಾಡಿಕೊಂಡೆ | **Māḍkoṇḍe** | I did it (for myself) |
| **Past (It)** | **-koṇḍitu** | ಮಾಡಿಕೊಂಡಿತು | **Māḍkoṇḍide** | It did it (It is doing) |
| **Future (I)** | **-koḷḷuttēne** | ಮಾಡಿಕೊಳ್ಳುತ್ತೇನೆ | **Māḍkotīni** | I will do it |
| **Imperative** | **-kō / -koḷḷi** | ಮಾಡಿಕೋ / ಕೊಳ್ಳಿ | **Māḍkō / Māḍkoḷḷi** | Do it! (Casual/Polite) |
| **Continuous** | **-koṇḍiddēne** | ಮಾಡಿಕೊಂಡು ಇದ್ದೇನೆ | **Māḍkoṇḍiddīni** | I am doing it (busy with) |
