
# A Grammar Guide to Conjunctions
**(Connecting Your Thoughts)**

## 1. Overview

In Kannada, connectors usually come between two full sentences to link ideas together.

---

## 2. The Basic Connectors: And, Or, But

### A. And: ಮತ್ತು (Mattu) / ಹಾಗೂ (Hāgū)
* **Mattu:** The standard word for "And." Used to connect nouns or sentences.
* **Hāgū:** Means "As well as" or "Also." It is slightly more formal or emphatic.

**Spoken Tip:** In fast conversation, people often drop *Mattu*. Instead, they use **Jotege** (With/Along with) or just pause.

| Context | Kannada | Transliteration | Meaning |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Nouns** | ಕಾಫಿ ಮತ್ತು ಟೀ. | **Kāphi mattu ṭī.** | Coffee and tea. |
| **Sentences** | ನಾನು ಬಂದೆ ಮತ್ತು ಅವಳು ಹೋದಳು. | **Nānu bande mattu avaḷu hōdaḷu.** | I came and she left. |

### B. Or: ಅಥವಾ (Athavā)
* **Hindi Equivalent:** *Ya / Athva*
* **Usage:** Used to present choices.

| Context | Kannada | Transliteration | Meaning |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Choice** | ಕಾಫಿ ಅಥವಾ ಟೀ ಬೇಕಾ? | **Kāphi athavā ṭī bēkā?** | Do you want Coffee or Tea? |
| **Plan** | ನಾಳೆ ಅಥವಾ ನಾಡಿದ್ದು. | **Nāḷe athavā nāḍiddu.** | Tomorrow or day-after. |

### C. But: ಆದರೆ (Ādare)
* **Hindi Equivalent:** *Lekin / Par*
* **Grammar Note:** Technically, *Ādare* means "If it becomes" (Conditional of *Āgu*), but it is the standard word for "But."

| Context | Kannada | Transliteration | Meaning |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Contrast** | ನನಗೆ ಇಷ್ಟ, ಆದರೆ ಸಮಯ ಇಲ್ಲ. | **Nanage iṣṭa, ādare samaya illa.** | I like it, but I don't have time. |
| **Fact** | ಇದು ಹಳೆಯದು, ಆದರೆ ಚೆನ್ನಾಗಿದೆ. | **Idu haḷeyadu, ādare cennāgide.** | This is old, but it is nice. |

## 3. The Logic Loop: Reason and Result

This is the most important section for daily conversation (explaining *why* you did something).

### A. Because: ಯಾಕೆಂದರೆ (Yākendare)
* **Spoken Variations:** *Yākandre / Yāke-andre*.
* **Literal Meaning:** "Why... means..." (*Yāke* + *Endare*).
* **Structure:** `[Result Sentence]` + `[Yākandre]` + `[Reason Sentence]`

| Kannada Sentence | Breakdown | Meaning |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **ನಾನು ಲೇಟ್, ಯಾಕೆಂದರೆ ಟ್ರಾಫಿಕ್ ಇತ್ತು.**<br>(Nānu lēṭ, yākandre ṭrāphik ittu.) | I am late, why-means traffic was there. | I am late because there was traffic. |
| **ಅವನು ಬರಲ್ಲ, ಯಾಕೆಂದರೆ ಅವನಿಗೆ ಹುಷಾರಿಲ್ಲ.**<br>(Avanu baralla, yākandre avanige huṣārilla.) | He won't come, why-means he is unwell. | He won't come because he is unwell. |

### B. So / Therefore: ಅದಕ್ಕೆ (Adakke)
* **Formal Version:** *Āddarinda* (Therefore).
* **Spoken Version:** *Adakke* (To that / For that reason).
* **Structure:** `[Reason Sentence]` + `[Adakke]` + `[Result Sentence]`

| Kannada Sentence | Breakdown | Meaning |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **ಟ್ರಾಫಿಕ್ ಇತ್ತು, ಅದಕ್ಕೆ ನಾನು ಲೇಟ್.**<br>(Ṭrāphik ittu, adakke nānu lēṭ.) | Traffic was there, to-that I am late. | There was traffic, so/that's why I am late. |
| **ನನಗೆ ಹಸಿವು, ಅದಕ್ಕೆ ಊಟ ಮಾಡಿದೆ.**<br>(Nanage hasivu, adakke ūṭa māḍide.) | Hunger to me, that's why I did meal. | I was hungry, so I ate. |

## 4. The "If Not / Otherwise" Connector

This is very common in Bengaluru Kannada when making plans.

### ಇಲ್ಲಾಂದ್ರೆ (Illāndre)
* **Literal:** *Illa* (No) + *Andre* (If said/meant).
* **Meaning:** Otherwise / If not / Or else.
* **Hindi Equivalent:** *Nahi toh*.

| Context | Kannada | Transliteration | Meaning |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Warning** | ಈಗ ಬನ್ನಿ, ಇಲ್ಲಾಂದ್ರೆ ನಾವು ಹೋಗ್ತೀವಿ. | **Īga banni, illāndre nāvu hōgtīvi.** | Come now, otherwise we will go. |
| **Alternative** | ನಾಳೆ ಸಿಗೋಣ, ಇಲ್ಲಾಂದ್ರೆ ಫೋನ್ ಮಾಡಿ. | **Nāḷe sigōṇa, illāndre phōn māḍi.** | Let's meet tomorrow, if not, call me. |

---

## 5. Summary: The Conversation Flow

Here is how you string it all together.

1.  **Start a thought:**
    * *Nanage bekkugaḷu iṣṭa.* (I like cats.)
2.  **Contrast (Ādare):**
    * *...ādare nanage bekku illa.* (...but I don't have a cat.)
3.  **Reason (Yākandre):**
    * *...yākandre nanna apārṭmeṇṭ cikkadu.* (...because my apartment is small.)
4.  **Result (Adakke):**
    * *...adakke nānu sadyakke tagoṇḍilla.* (...so I haven't taken one **for now**.)
